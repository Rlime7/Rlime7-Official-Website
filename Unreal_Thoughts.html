Unreal Thoughts, my first EP...
Consisting of four songs (two really,lol) is a very dark and ominous piece of ambient music with some minimal drums here and there. It was heavily inspired by some genre that I discovered at the beginning of 2023. Here are some examples: https://youtu.be/vnDuuvqCiWk , https://youtu.be/w-oYzba5iF4
Theese two are one of my favourites and I kind of wanted to achieve that...Did I manage to achieve that? I don't know, you tell me. Anyways, my personal favourite is `This isn't real`, as it represents the best this genre.
I started working on this somewhere in late January 2023 and kind of just let the files waiting in there until this moment...
